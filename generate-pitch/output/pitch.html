🎯 Key Innovation: The Reel Experience

Our platform replicates the exact format that keeps users scrolling for hours on TikTok and Instagram. But instead of entertainment, every video is a learning moment.

📱 How The Reel Works

The user swipes left on a flashcard they don't know. Instantly, they're transported into a vertical video feed - identical to Instagram Reels. Each video shows:

  • Full-screen vertical video of the concept (e.g., a dog running, playing, barking)
  • Word overlay in large text at the top: "HUND" (German for dog)
  • Translation subtitle at the bottom: "dog"
  • Auto-play as soon as it appears on screen
  • Swipe up/down to see the next video - no friction, no loading

The brain associates the visual stimulus (dog video) with the word overlay (HUND) through repetition and dopamine. After 8 videos showing different dogs in different contexts, all with "HUND" overlaid, the word is burned into memory.

🧠 Why This Works: The Psychology

  • Visual Association: The brain links the moving image with the word overlay instantly
  • Repetition Without Boredom: 8 different videos of dogs = 8 exposures to "HUND" without feeling repetitive
  • Context Learning: Seeing dogs in different situations (playing, running, sleeping) reinforces the concept
  • Zero Friction: Swipe up = instant next video. No clicks, no loading, no thinking
  • Dopamine Loop: Each video is a mini-reward. The brain craves "just one more"
  • Muscle Memory: Users already know how to use this format - they do it for hours on social media
